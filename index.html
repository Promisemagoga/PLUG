Understanding the NQF level certificates

  • NQF level 1 = Grade 9(General Education certificate)
  • NQF level 2 = Grade 10(Elementary certificate)
  • NQF level 3 = Grade 11(Intermidiate certificate)
  • NQF level 4 = Grade 12(National Senior certificate)
  • NQF level 5 = (Higher certificate)
  • NQF level 6 = (Diploma or Advanced certificate)
  • NQF level 7 = (Bachelors Degree or Advanced Diploma)
  • NQF level 8 = (Honours Degree)
  • NQF level 9 = (Master Degree)
  • NQF level 10 = (Doctorates Degree)
